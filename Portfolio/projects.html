Smart Irrigation

Smart Irrigation is an AI-powered solution designed to optimize water usage in agriculture and gardening. Traditional irrigation methods often waste water and fail to adapt to real-time environmental conditions. With Smart Irrigation, sensors and intelligent algorithms work together to deliver the right amount of water at the right time, improving efficiency and crop health.

Auto Mind

AutoMind is a data-driven machine learning project designed to predict the resale price of cars with high accuracy. It leverages historical datasets containing details such as brand, model, year of manufacture, fuel type, mileage, engine capacity, and other features to estimate the fair market value of a vehicle. Using Python, Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, and Matplotlib, the project involves data preprocessing, feature engineering, model training, and evaluation.
